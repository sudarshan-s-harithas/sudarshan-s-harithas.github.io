Sudarshan S Harithas

Hey there!!!, Thank you for visiting my page. I am an MS by Research Student at IIIT Hyderabad, supervised by Dr. K Madhava Krishna in the Robotics Research Center. My research studies the interplay between scene representation and robotic action. Broady I have developed compact and effective representations of 3D scenes such that it helps robots improve their decision-making abilities. I am particularly interested in solving problems related to uncertain and dynamic environments

I completed my Bachelor's in Electronics and Communication from BMS College of Engineering.
